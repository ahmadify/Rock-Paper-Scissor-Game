# ✊✋✌️ Rock-Paper-Scissors Game

Welcome to my **Rock-Paper-Scissors** web game! Play against the computer and see if you can outsmart it.  

Try it live here: [View Live Game](https://ahmadify.github.io/Rock-Paper-Scissor-Game/)

---

## Features

- **Play against the computer** – Randomized moves make each game exciting.  
- **Score tracking** – Keep track of wins, losses, and draws.  
- **Interactive UI** – Buttons for rock, paper, and scissors.  
- **Responsive design** – Works on desktop and mobile devices.  
- **Simple and fun** – Lightweight and easy to play.  

---

## How to Play

1. Click one of the buttons: Rock, Paper, or Scissors.  
2. The computer will choose a move randomly.  
3. The winner of the round is displayed.  
4. Play as many rounds as you want and see your score.  

---

## Technologies Used

- **HTML5** – Structure of the game.  
- **CSS3** – Styling, animations, and responsive design.  
- **JavaScript** – Game logic, randomization, and score tracking.
